We offer two peer support groups: The Mid-Peninsula Brain Injury Peer Support Group and the Mid-Peninsula Concussion Peer Support Group. The purpose of these groups is to support individuals with brain injury and post-concussion syndrome in the mid-peninsula community. Peer support group meetings provide opportunities for individuals and caregivers to connect and learn from one another by sharing experiences, resources, and advice related to brain injury and the recovery process. These meetings also foster friendships and a sense of community among members. Our peer support group meetings are confidential and led by trained student moderators. Every so often, we invite guest speakers in the brain injury field to give a talk and lead a discussion at peer support group meetings.

Mid-Peninsula Brain Injury Peer Support Group

This general peer support group welcomes individuals with acquired brain injury (including concussion) and their friends and family. This group meets twice-monthly; the meeting dates can be found on our Events calendar.

Mid-Peninsula Concussion Peer Support Group

This concussion-specific peer support group welcomes individuals with concussion and their friends and family. This group meets monthly; the meeting dates can be found on our Events calendar.
