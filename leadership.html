Vice President of Events

Sophie loves working with the events committee to help bring individuals with brain injury together for both social events and guest speakers that can be enjoyed with families and student buddies. Sophie’s favorite activities include fishing, painting, baking, and skiing.

Divya is excited to expand support for individuals with brain injury through SSBI’s programs. She is interested in peer-to-peer interaction as a way to support emotional well-being. In her free time, she enjoys running, dancing, and reading!

Claire is thrilled to be involved in SSBI and hopes to facilitate the development of meaningful friendships between individuals with brain injury and Stanford undergraduate students.
